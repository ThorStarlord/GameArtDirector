# Asset Types

Use these additions automatically when the request implies a specific asset class.

## Character

Add silhouette readability, pose clarity, role, faction cues, costume hierarchy, and gameplay-friendly proportions.

## Enemy

Add threat level, aggression read, weak-point clarity, combat hierarchy, and instant recognition against likely environments.

## Weapon

Add material definition, grip or edge readability, damage fantasy, faction styling, and pickup or icon readability.

## Prop

Add gameplay purpose, interaction cues, scale clarity, and simplified forms that read quickly during play.

## Building

Add architectural style, intended function, entrance readability, structural rhythm, and how it belongs in the world.

## Environment Piece

Add foreground-background separation, traversal clarity, tiling requirements when appropriate, and consistent lighting logic.

## UI Element

Add usability, modular construction, frame hierarchy, empty-state clarity, and readability at gameplay resolution.

## Icon

Add instant recognition at small sizes, a single dominant silhouette, centered composition, and controlled detail.

## VFX

Add motion implication, energy shape, timing feel, impact readability, and clear separation from the background.
